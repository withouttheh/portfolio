About Me

Luto Sibotoboto is a self-taught Front-End Developer who works with HTML, CSS & Javascript to create rich, user-friendly and interactive websites for personal and professional brands.
After spending time in the world of construction, Luto knows what it takes to deliver quality projects according to clients specifications - and it is not just about technical skills. It's how well you connect with the heart-beating people you're trying to help - communicating and visualising their ideas.
He is driven by his passion for all things tech and derives joy from helping people and communities improve their lives through tech. In addition to his love for programming, Luto is a qualified Civil Engineer. He takes up social running as a hobby, competing in half-marathon and marathon events. My goal is to one day run the Comrades Marathon 90km Race.

Luto holds a National Diploma in Civil Engineering from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology.

He was born and raised in East London, South Africa.
